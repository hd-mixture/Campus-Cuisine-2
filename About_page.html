Darshan Prajapati

CEO & Co-founder

Campus Cuisine is a food discovery and delivery platform designed to make affordable and accessible meals available to college students. Launched in March 2024, the platform connects students with campus vendors, allowing them to explore meal options, place pre-orders, and access exclusive discounts. With a focus on convenience and affordability, Campus Cuisine ensures students get quality food without long wait times.

The platform is being developed using:

  • Front-end: HTML, JavaScript, CSS
  • Back-end: Node.js
  • Database: MongoDB
